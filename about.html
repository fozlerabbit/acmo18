About ACMO

Empowering communities to end child marriage through education, awareness, and youth-led initiatives

Our Story

From a small group of passionate youth to a movement that's changing lives

2020

The Beginning

ACMO was founded by a group of passionate young activists in Sirajganj who witnessed the devastating effects of child marriage in their communities. What started as informal discussions became a structured organization committed to creating change.

2021

First Programs

We launched our first community awareness programs, reaching over 500 families across 5 villages. Our focus on education and community engagement started showing promising results with local support growing significantly.

2022

Expansion

Our programs expanded to cover 15 districts across Bangladesh. We formed partnerships with local NGOs and government agencies, establishing ACMO as a recognized voice in the fight against child marriage.

2023

Recognition

ACMO received national recognition for our innovative approaches to preventing child marriage. We were invited to participate in policy discussions and became a member of Girls Not Brides Bangladesh coalition.

2024
2024

Strategic Partnership

We signed a groundbreaking MoU with ScriptySphere, bringing technology-driven solutions to our mission. This partnership enables us to scale our impact through innovative digital platforms and data-driven approaches.

Our Mission

To eliminate child marriage in Bangladesh through comprehensive education programs, community engagement, policy advocacy, and youth-led initiatives that empower girls and transform communities.

Our Vision

A Bangladesh where every girl has the freedom to pursue her dreams, complete her education, and choose her own path in life without the threat of child marriage.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Education First

We believe education is the most powerful tool for breaking the cycle of poverty and preventing child marriage.

Community-Centered

Real change happens at the grassroots level through active community participation and ownership.

Youth Leadership

Young people are powerful agents of change who can drive social transformation in their communities.

Gender Equality

We work towards a society where girls and boys have equal opportunities and rights.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of partnerships and work closely with communities, organizations, and government.

Evidence-Based

Our programs are informed by research, data, and continuous monitoring and evaluation.

Our Approach

We use a multi-faceted approach that addresses the root causes of child marriage

01

Community Education

Comprehensive awareness programs that educate communities about the harmful effects of child marriage and the benefits of girls' education.

02

Youth Empowerment

Training young leaders to become advocates and change agents in their own communities.

03

Economic Support

Providing economic opportunities and support to families to reduce the financial incentives for child marriage.

04

Policy Advocacy

Working with government and policymakers to strengthen laws and policies that protect children.
